Cosmopolitan Cocktail

A Cosmopolitan Cocktail is a classic alcoholic drink that makes the list of International Bartender Association’s IBA official cocktails. The history of the Cosmo is disputed: some claim it was invented in the 1930’s, others in the 1970’s.

What’s in a Cosmo? The classic version of this cocktail uses Citron vodka, cranberry juice (usually sweetened), Cointreau, and lime. Why make our version of this classic? Keep reading.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of vodka or citron vodka
  • 2 tablespoons of 100% cranberry juice
  • 1 tablespoon of Cointreu
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1 lime wedge
  • 1 teaspoon simple syrup or maple syrup

Steps

  1. Place the vodka, cranberry juice, Cointreau, lemon juice, and syrup in a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Shake 15 seconds until cold. Strain the liquid into a martini glass.
  3. Squeeze with the lime wedge and serve, garnished with a lime wheel if desired.
